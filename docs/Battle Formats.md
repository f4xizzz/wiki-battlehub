# **Server Config**

---

## **Battle Formats and Presets**

**Cobblemon BattleHUB** has native support for multiple competitive and casual battle formats managed by the internal logic of the `RankedPreset` class. These formats define arena behavior, how many Pokémon enter the field simultaneously, and the structural battle rules.

---

## **Supported Base Formats**

The mod adapts dynamically to the official rules of the Pokémon eSports ecosystem, supporting three main battle structures:

* **Singles (Individual):** Traditional 1v1 battles.  
* **Doubles:** 2v2 battles (the standard format for official VGC).  
* **Triples:** 3v3 battles with higher strategic complexity.

---

## **Rule Presets (`RankedPreset`)**

To simplify management and ensure competitive balance in ranked queues, the mod includes **Rule Presets** pre-configured directly in the code.

Below is the specification for each preset available in the mod:

### **1. Solo (`solo`)**

* **Battle Structure:** Singles (1v1)  
* **Level Cap:** 50  
* **Species Clause:** Enabled (Prevents duplicate Pokémon on the same team)  
* **Item Clause:** Enabled (Prevents duplicate held items)  
* **Showdown Rules:** `Standard` (Ensures Smogon competitive base validations)

### **2. Doubles (`doubles`)**

* **Battle Structure:** Doubles (2v2)  
* **Level Cap:** 50  
* **Species Clause:** Enabled  
* **Item Clause:** Enabled  
* **Showdown Rules:** `Standard`

### **3. Triples (`triples`)**

* **Battle Structure:** Triples (3v3)  
* **Level Cap:** 50  
* **Species Clause:** Enabled  
* **Item Clause:** Enabled  
* **Showdown Rules:** `Standard`

### **4. Monotype (`monotype`)**

* **Battle Structure:** Singles (1v1)  
* **Level Cap:** 50  
* **Species Clause:** Enabled  
* **Item Clause:** Enabled  
* **Showdown Rules:** `Standard` and `Same Type Clause` (Requires all Pokémon on the team to share at least one elemental type).

### **5. Custom (`custom`)**

* **Battle Structure:** Singles (1v1) by default.  
* **Level Cap:** 50 by default.  
* **Species Clause:** Disabled by default.  
* **Item Clause:** Disabled by default.  
* **Description:** Allows administrators to create fully custom rules in Ladder files, ignoring the mod's standard competitive restrictions.

---

## **Gimmicks and Arena Mechanics**

Regardless of the selected rule preset, the mod allows you to enable or disable the main battle mechanics introduced across Pokémon generations in Ladder configurations:

* **Mega Evolution** (`allowMega`)  
* **Z-Moves** (`allowZMove`)  
* **Dynamax / Gigantamax** (`allowDynamax`)  
* **Terastal** (`allowTera`)

!!! tip "Performance Optimization"
    All of the above mechanics are applied when creating the `BattleFormat` object generated by the mod, ensuring Cobblemon's battle engine validates and applies mechanics in real time without causing desynchronization between client and server.
